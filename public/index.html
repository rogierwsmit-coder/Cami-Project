About

CAMI is a wearable personal safety platform created to increase the sense of safety for women in public spaces. The product combines a body-worn device (camera, sensors, alarm) with a mobile app and a subscription service. CAMI focuses not only on incident response, but especially on prevention, peace of mind, and direct support when a situation escalates.
